How ordering works

Ordering a handmade piece is easy and personal, here's exactly how it goes.

1

Browse & pick

Add your favourites to the cart, or fill the custom form with your own idea.

2

Send on WhatsApp

Your order reaches me in one message. I confirm availability and the final price.

3

Made & delivered

I handcraft it with love, share little updates, and pack it safely for you.

How long does an order take?

Ready pieces are usually dispatched in a few days. Custom & resin pieces take a little longer as each one is made by hand, I'll always share a timeline on WhatsApp.

Do you ship across India?

Yes! Orders are shipped pan-India. Delivery charges depend on your location and are confirmed when you message.

How does payment work?

There's no payment on the website. Once you send your order, I confirm the details and share UPI / payment info personally.

Can I personalise my order?

Absolutely, that's my favourite part. Names, photos, colours, dried flowers, varmala preservation and more. Use the custom order form and tell me your idea.

How do I care for resin pieces?

Keep them away from direct sunlight and harsh heat, and wipe gently with a soft dry cloth. They'll stay beautiful for years.

Can I return or exchange?

As each piece is handmade (and often personalised), returns aren't possible, but if anything arrives damaged, message me right away and I'll make it right. 💛
